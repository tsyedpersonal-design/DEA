Our Mission

The Dallas Entrepreneurial Alliance was founded on a simple belief: that great ideas have no age limit, and that the entrepreneurial spirit is alive across every corner of the Dallas–Fort Worth community.

We bring world-class business leaders into the same room as DFW's most ambitious people, to create a direct line between those with deep experience and those with bold aspiration.

Come ready to listen, ask hard questions, and leave with a clearer vision of what's possible.

Sports & Brand Marketing
Jerry Jones Jr.
Executive Vice President
Dallas Cowboys · NFL

One of the most influential executives in professional sports, Jerry Jones Jr. serves as Chief Sales & Marketing Officer of the Dallas Cowboys — the most valuable sports franchise on the planet, valued at over $9 billion.

Jerry Jr. has spent his career at the intersection of sports, entertainment, and business, helping transform the Cowboys from a football team into a global media and lifestyle brand. He has overseen landmark sponsorship deals, the development of AT&T Stadium — one of the world's most sophisticated sports venues — and the Cowboys' evolution into a year-round entertainment property. Beyond the Cowboys, Jerry Jr. has been deeply involved in the Jones family's broader business empire, including ownership interests in real estate and other ventures. He is a graduate of Georgetown University and has long been recognized as a driving force behind the Cowboys' commercial dominance. His story is one of taking an already legendary brand and finding ways to make it bigger, bolder, and more relevant in an era of rapid change in media and culture — a challenge every entrepreneur can learn from.

Tech & Consumer Products
Mandy Ginsberg
Former CEO, Match Group
Tinder · Hinge

Mandy Ginsberg is one of the most decorated executives in the history of consumer technology. As CEO of Match Group — the parent company of Match.com, Tinder, OKCupid, Hinge, and dozens of other dating platforms — she oversaw a portfolio reaching hundreds of millions of users in over 190 countries.

Mandy spent nearly two decades at Match Group, rising from VP of Marketing to President of Match North America, then CEO of Match.com, before taking the helm as CEO of the entire Match Group portfolio. She is widely credited with modernizing Match.com's brand during a critical era of competition, and with building a culture of data-driven product development across the company's many brands. Under her leadership, Match Group's revenue grew substantially, and Tinder became one of the most downloaded apps in the world. A graduate of the University of Texas at Austin and Northwestern University's Kellogg School of Management, Mandy has been named one of Fortune's Most Powerful Women in Business. She is also known as a passionate advocate for women in leadership, for authentic company culture, and for the kind of gritty, roll-up-your-sleeves work ethic that defines great entrepreneurs. She now serves on several company boards and is an active mentor to the next generation of business leaders.

Clean Energy & Innovation
Kevin Fine
Co-Founder, Lunar Batteries

Kevin Fine is a clean energy entrepreneur who played a pivotal role in one of the most consequential product launches in modern technology — the Tesla Powerwall. As the head of Tesla's Powerwall division, he helped bring home battery storage from a futuristic concept to a product in millions of households.

At Tesla, Kevin was part of the energy division during a transformational period — when Elon Musk's team was racing to prove that sustainable energy at home wasn't just possible, but desirable and accessible. The Powerwall became a symbol of a broader movement: the idea that clean energy could be beautiful, simple, and empowering for everyday families. After Tesla, Kevin brought that same vision to Lunar Energy, a next-generation home energy company building integrated solar, battery, and smart home systems designed to give homeowners complete energy independence. Lunar Energy has raised significant venture funding and is widely regarded as one of the most exciting companies in the clean energy space. Kevin's career is a compelling example of how entrepreneurial thinking is reshaping one of the world's most important industries — and how the decisions made in boardrooms and garages today will determine the planet's future. For anyone passionate about climate, technology, or building things that matter, Kevin's perspective is invaluable.
